Career Path Recommendations

Based on your experience and interests, here are some potential career tracks and details on each:

Investment Banking Analyst

Pros: High earning potential, fast-paced environment, work on high-profile deals.

Cons: Long hours (80+ hrs/week), high stress.

Salary: Starting $100k+ and bonuses.

Work-life balance: Poor, expect to work very long hours.

Career potential: Associate, VP, MD roles. Exit ops in PE, hedge funds, corporate roles.

Data Scientist

Pros: Use advanced analytics, work on impactful projects, high demand for skills.

Cons: Can be isolating/lonely work. Need to constantly learn new skills and tools.

Salary: $100k-$130k for entry level roles.

Work-life balance: Average to good work-life balance.

Career potential: Lead data scientist, head of analytics, CTO.

Software Engineer

Pros: Competitive salary, good benefits, engaging technical work.

Cons: Requires constant learning and skill development. Can be stressful.

Salary: $100k-$140k for entry level at top tech firms.

Work-life balance: Average, depends on team and company culture.

Career potential: Senior engineer, tech lead, CTO.

Management Consulting Analyst

Pros: Variety of projects, work with top executives, fast career progression, develop business strategy skills.

Cons: Frequent travel and long hours. High pressure to perform.

Salary: $80k-$90k starting.

Work-life balance: Below average, expect 60-80 hr weeks.

Career potential: Progress from analyst to consultant to manager/partner. Exit ops in industry.

Digital Marketing Manager

Pros: Play a key role in building and enhancing a company's online presence.

Cons: The industry evolves quickly, you'll need to stay updated on industry trends and technologies. Can be competitive.

Salary: Starting between $60k and $100k per year.

Work-life balance: Average, depends on company and role.

Career potential: Sr Digital Marketing Manager, Director of Digital Marketing, CMO

Product Manager

Pros: Strategic role, work cross-functionally, high impact on business.

Cons: Can be ambiguous, fast-paced, lots of accountability.

Salary: $100k-$150k starting compensation at top tech companies.

Work-life balance: Can be good or bad depending on company and role demands.

Career potential: Senior PM, Director of Product, VP of Product.
