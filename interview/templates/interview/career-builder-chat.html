What does your day-to-day look like?

A typical day of mine is characterized by rigorous and fast-paced activities. Normally I arrive in office between 8-9am. After immediately check emails and voicemails to prioritize tasks for the day, I attend the morning meeting with the team to get updates on deals and assignments. My daily duty includes: to work on valuation analyses and pitch decks for current deals and pitches; to build, validate and maintain financial models; to synthesize analysis into presentation format. I work 10-12 hours per day on average. My hours fluctuate with deals and markets, and can be worse when nearing big deadlines
